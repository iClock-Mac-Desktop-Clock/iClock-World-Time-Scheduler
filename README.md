## What is iClock?

![iClock World Time View](https://encrypted-tbn0.gstatic.com/images?q=tbn:ANd9GcSNg4DBV6QafZIDgtpS82swK79m4snE8mb_RQ&s)

iClock is a powerful yet simple desktop application that helps users manage time across different zones and stay organized with built-in scheduling tools. It offers a clean, always-visible clock display alongside world time tracking, making it easier to coordinate with international teams, plan meetings, or keep track of multiple locations.

With iClock, you can customize the look and position of the clock, set alarms, run timers, and even display multiple time zones at once. The app integrates seamlessly into your macOS desktop environment without interrupting your workflow. Its minimalist design ensures it blends beautifully with your setup while providing essential time-related functions at a glance.

iClock is built for modern macOS systems, including support for Apple Silicon and Intel Macs. It works flawlessly on macOS Sonoma, Ventura, Monterey, and older versions. The app is lightweight, secure, and does not require an internet connection for basic functions, ensuring your data stays private.
